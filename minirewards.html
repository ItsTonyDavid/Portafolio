MiniRewards

This project is still being under development.

Website for small shops to be able to have their own rewards software for their loyaer customers. Giving them the oportunity to make promotions or gifts.

Startup being developed by 3 friends and me. We are trying to: Digitalizate: Eliminate the use of rewards cards.
Reachable: Our business model make it reachable to local stores to own their own rewards sfotware.
Make a change: Many local stores lose customers because what they sell can be easily repeated. With our software, the customer retention will increase.
